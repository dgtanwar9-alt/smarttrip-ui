✦ Getting Around

Transport Guide

Everything you need to know about reaching and navigating your destination.

✈️
By Air
Jaipur International Airport
Located 13km from city centre. Flights from Delhi (1hr), Mumbai (2hrs), Bengaluru (2.5hrs). Terminal 2 for domestic flights.
Airport to City ₹400–₹700 (cab)
🚂
By Rail
Jaipur Railway Station
Well connected to Delhi (5hrs by Shatabdi), Jodhpur (5hrs), Mumbai (18hrs). Pre-paid autos available outside station.
Station to Hotel ₹80–₹200 (auto)
🚌
Local Transport
Bus / Metro / Auto
City buses cover major routes (₹10–₹30). Autos easily available. App-based cabs (Ola/Uber) reliable. No metro yet — bus is best for budget travel.
Avg Local Trip ₹50–₹150
🛺
Rickshaw & Taxi
Cycle Rickshaw & E-Rickshaw
Best for Old City navigation. Cycle rickshaws are iconic — negotiate before boarding. E-rickshaws available near major tourist spots.
Short Ride ₹20–₹60

Hidden Gems

📍 Secret Viewpoint
Nahargarh Fort Sunset
Most tourists crowd Amber Fort, but the Nahargarh rampart walk offers a 360° view of Jaipur's pink skyline at golden hour — nearly empty!
📌 Jaipur Free Entry
🌿 Quiet
☕ Quiet Cafe
Peacock Rooftop, Udaipur
Tucked in a haveli lane with a postcard-perfect view of Lake Pichola and City Palace. No crowds, live sitar music, simple Rajasthani food.
📌 Udaipur Local Fav
🛍 Hidden
🛒 Local Market
Tripolia Bazaar Lane
Skip Johari Bazaar — Tripolia's inner lanes hide master craftsmen selling silver jewellery, lac bangles and hand-block fabric at half the price.
📌 Jaipur Best Price
📸 Photo Spot
📷 Photography Spot
Mehrangarh Blue Steps
A hidden staircase inside Jodhpur's old city painted in 50 shades of blue — an Instagrammer's paradise that even most locals don't know!
📌 Jodhpur Insta-worthy
🌅 Secret
🌄 Secret Beach
Butterfly Beach, Goa
Accessible only by boat from Agonda, this crescent cove is dolphin territory — arrive at 6am and you'll share it with just the dolphins!
📌 South Goa Secluded
✨ Local
🎭 Local Experience
Galta Ji Monkey Temple
A 5km trek from Jaipur through Aravalli hills reveals a 17th-century monkey temple — sacred bathing tanks, 1000 resident monkeys and zero tourist buses!
📌 Jaipur Outskirts Rare Gem
